Your Journey, Your Way: Approaches to Wellness

We offer compassionate, personalized guidance using proven approaches to help you feel heard, understood, and supported:



  • CBT & DBT: Shift unhelpful thoughts and build coping skills
  • Somatic Therapy: Release stress stored in the body
  • IFS & Narrative Therapy: Explore your inner self and reframe your story
  • Person-Centered Therapy: Grow through empathy and acceptance
  • Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT): Strengthen connections and relationships
